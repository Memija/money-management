# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to be **good humans by default**. We are committed to making participation in our community a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

We act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, inclusive, and healthy project environment.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment for our community include:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing to those affected by our mistakes, and learning from the experience
- Focusing on what is best not just for us as individuals, but for the overall community

---

### Reporting Issues

If you have any concerns or need to report unacceptable behavior, please reach out to the project maintainers through the application's contact form.
